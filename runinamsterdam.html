Run in Amsterdam

Where to go for a run in Amsterdam?

Amsterdamse Bos

Amsterdamse Bos is a vast green space ideal for runners, offering over 50 kilometers of scenic trails through forests, meadows, and alongside tranquil lakes. Popular for both casual joggers and serious athletes, the park provides diverse routes, from flat paths to challenging inclines. Its natural beauty and peaceful surroundings make it a perfect escape from the city's hustle, offering a refreshing environment for running year-round. Whether you're training for a marathon or enjoying a leisurely jog, Amsterdamse Bos is a runner's haven in the heart of nature.

Westerpark

Westerpark is a vibrant green space in Amsterdam, popular with runners for its mix of open fields, wooded paths, and scenic water features. The park’s well-maintained trails and peaceful atmosphere make it ideal for both casual joggers and serious runners. With cultural events, cafes, and art installations along the way, it offers a dynamic running experience in the heart of the city.

Vondelpark

Vondelpark, Amsterdam’s largest and most famous park, is a top destination for runners, offering flat, paved paths through lush greenery. The 5-kilometer loop around the park is popular for its scenic views of ponds, bridges, and historic statues. Its central location and vibrant atmosphere, with cafes and outdoor performances, make it a lively spot for both casual and seasoned runners.
